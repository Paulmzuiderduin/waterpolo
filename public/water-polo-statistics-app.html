Water Polo Statistics App

Waterpolo Hub provides a practical water polo statistics app for season and match analysis.

Core statistics

  • Team totals for shots, goals, fouls, turnovers, and timeouts
  • Player statistics by cap number and event type
  • Stat sheet view by match or whole season
  • Shot conversion and event trend summaries

Built for coaches

Data is organized by season and team, so each staff can keep a clean workspace for match prep and review.
