Water Polo Stat Sheet App

Waterpolo Hub includes a water polo stat sheet app for player-by-player and team match reporting.

What you can do

  • Generate per-player stat sheets by match or full season
  • Export reports directly to XLSX for coaches and team staff
  • Import standardized CSV/XLSX templates into scoring data
  • Review event totals like goals, shots, fouls, and turnovers

Recommended workflow

Log live actions in the Scoring module during matches, then open Stat Sheet for clean match and season reports.
